The Bottom Line

You Don't Need a $200 Grooming Subscription

A good self-cleaning brush, a quiet nail grinder, the right dental treats, and a stash of high-value bribes will get you 90% of the way there — and turn what used to be a stressful monthly battle into a low-key Sunday routine. My couch is fur-free (mostly), my dog's nails don't click across the hardwood at 2am anymore, and nobody's breath makes me want to leave the room.

If you're currently buried in fur, dreading nail trim day, or side-eyeing your dog's breath — just start with one thing on this list. Future you, and your couch, will thank you.
