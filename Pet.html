The Bottom Line

None of this happened overnight, and I definitely didn't buy everything at once. I started with the hair roller because that was driving me the most crazy, then added the rest over a couple of months as each new annoyance came up. If you're only tackling one thing this week, start with whatever's bothering you the most — shedding, skin, scratching, or water bowl grossness — and go from there.

Pet parenting isn't about doing everything perfectly. It's about finding the handful of small fixes that make the daily grind easier — for you and for them.
